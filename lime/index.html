Use + / − to stack any combination of LimePass bundles. Each bundle only covers usage inside its validity window (1 / 3 / 7 / 30 days), so a 7-day pass can cover at most one week's rides — the line is colour-coded by which pass is being consumed at each point, with a dot at every transition. Buying more bundles than fit in a month (e.g. 2× 30D) wastes the extras. Find optimal combo brute-forces the cheapest realistic stack for your rides/avg. Unused minutes are valued at the combo's blended per-minute rate. London PAYG rates as of the November 2025 hike (£1 unlock + 31p/min, £1 flat under 5 min).
